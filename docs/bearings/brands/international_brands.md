# International bearing brands

## Classification by price and quality

### High price & high quality
- **SKF** (Sweden) – the most well‑known bearing brand with a very wide product range; renowned for reliability and high price.
- **FAG/INA** (Germany, Schaeffler Group) – top‑tier quality comparable to SKF, broad assortment, premium pricing.
- **NTN‑SNR** (Japan/France) – high‑quality bearings; some designs are complex and expensive.
- **Koyo** (Japan) – precision bearings for automotive and industrial use; high quality at a slightly lower price than SKF and FAG.
- **IKO** (Japan) – specialized manufacturer of high‑precision needle and roller bearings.
- **Timken** (USA) – premium tapered roller and spherical bearings.
- **THK** (Japan) – high‑precision linear motion components and rolling bearings.
- **APB/IDC** (USA) – niche brand producing high‑performance bearings.

### Medium price & quality
- **NSK** (Japan) – offers a broad range of bearings at moderate prices.
- **NACHI / Nippon Bearing** (Japan) – good quality and mid‑range pricing.
- **FYH** (Japan) – quality bearing units at reasonable prices.
- **RHP** (United Kingdom/China, part of NSK) – mid‑range quality and pricing.
- **Various new Austrian‑Asian and Taiwanese‑German brands** – newcomers offering acceptable quality at mid‑level prices.

### Low price (budget segment)
- **TSC**, **ISB**, **KDF**, **NBS**, **LSK**, **FBJ** – low‑cost bearings produced mainly at contract factories in Asia; quality may vary.
- **FLT**, **DKF DDR** – older East‑European brands; now produced in Asia and positioned in the budget segment.

These classifications reflect general market positioning; actual quality can vary by product line. When selecting a brand, consider required load ratings, operating conditions and availability.
